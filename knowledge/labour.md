# Code on Wages, 2019

## Overview

The Code on Wages, 2019 is an Indian labour law that regulates the payment of wages, minimum wages, bonus, and equal remuneration for employees.

---

## Delayed Salary

An employer is legally expected to pay wages within the prescribed time under the Code on Wages, 2019.

If wages are not paid, an employee may seek legal remedies through the appropriate labour authority.

---

## Employee Rights

Employees have the right to:

* Receive wages on time.
* Receive at least the applicable minimum wages.
* Receive wages without unlawful deductions.
* File a complaint before the appropriate labour authority.

---

## Remedies

If salary is delayed, an employee may:

1. Contact the employer in writing.
2. Send a formal legal notice.
3. Approach the Labour Commissioner.
4. File a claim before the competent labour authority.
5. Seek legal assistance if the dispute continues.
